## Our pledge

We vibe together as a welcoming, harassment-free community. ## Enforcement responsibilities

Maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing our standards. Theywill take appropriate corrective action in response to unacceptable behavior.Enforcement decisions are communicated promptly and remain at the maintainers'discretion.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all community spaces, including GitHub issues,PRs, Discussions, docs, and synchronous channels. It also applies when anindividual is officially representing the project in public spaces.

## Enforcement

Issues can be reported to the maintainers at[mailto:lukefrankio@gmail.com](mailto:lukefrankio@gmail.com). All complaints will be reviewed promptly and fairly.The team respects reporters' confidentiality requests whenever legally possible.

Enforcement guidelines may include any of the following actions:

1. **Correction** — maintainers issue a private, written warning.
2. **Warning** — the community receives a warning with consequences forcontinued behavior.
3. **Temporary ban** — the contributor is temporarily removed from communityspaces.
4. **Permanent ban** — the contributor is removed from the community.
